WHAT IS THE INTERNATIONAL SPACE APPS CHALLENGE?

The 2014 International Space Apps Challenge is a technology development and problem solving event during which citizens from around the world collaborate to tackle challenges relevant to improving life on Earth and the exploration of space. Join us in 100 cities around the world or at home!

Organized by the New York Technology Council and StartupBus NYC.

STILL NOT CONVINCED?

Because NYC did a phenomenal job last year, NASA has designated New York City as the global mainstage site and will receive a special guest appearance from an actual NASA astronaut!

WHO SHOULD PARTICIPATE?

Developers, designers, thinkers, researchers, scientists and other technology-focused individuals.
