JOIN THE MOVEMENT

APRIL 19-21, 2013

Sign Up Today!

The International Space Apps Challenge is a technology development and problem solving event during which citizens from around the world collaborate to tackle challenges relevant to improving life on Earth and the exploration of space. Join us in over 75 cities around the world... or at home!

Organized by the New York Technology Council, StartupBus NYC and AlleyNYC.

{% include video.html %}

WHO SHOULD PARTICIPATE?

Developers, designers, thinkers, researchers, scientists, other related technically minded folks and anyone else who would be interested in building new technologies, generating fresh ideas, and creating thoughtful solutions that advance the state of space exploration missions, science, and social good on Earth. * Prior hacking not required!

WHAT SHOULD YOU EXPECT?

  • Expect a little confusion. (It's okay, we're in it together!)
  • Expect intense brainstorming.
  • Expect to learn about new technologies.
  • Expect to work on cool problems.
  • Expect little to no sleep. (If you like all-nighters!)
  • Expect to meet other enthusiastic and awesome people from your neighborhood and from all over the world!
  • FREE meals, drinks, and snacks all weekend. Swag & prizes, and a few surprises!
