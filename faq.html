FREQUENT QUESTIONS

YOUT GOT 'EM, WE'LL ANSWER THEM!

WE'LL TRY TO ANSWER ANY QUESTIONS YOU HAVE

When is the event taking place?
April 20-21, 2013

Is there a fee to participate?
No. This event is 100% FREE.

Is there an age requirement?
18 years or older. If you're under 18, please get permission from a responsible adult.

Is there a dress code?
No. Wear comfortable and preferably clean clothes.

Where is the event located?
Alley NYC, 500 Seventh Avenue, 13th Floor.

What time does the event start and end?
Things kick-off at 10:00AM, but come whenever you want. Please note that you need to be an ACTIVE participant on BOTH days in order to be eligible for prizes and freebies.

Can I watch the event online?
Yes. The event will be streamed on the internet.

Can I join a team?
Yes. If you don't have a team, come solo and make friends.

If I take a nap during the event, will I be disqualified?
Hackathons are very challenging and we understand if you need a little break.

How will my product be judged?
We will take into account creativity, ease of use, level of complexity, use of available space data, and team dynamics.

Who are the judges?
A team of experts from tech and science.

Will you feed me?
We will provide breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks, but you're welcome to bring your own food or purchase something in the surrounding area. Please note that if you buy your own food, it is at your own expense. We do not provide reimbursements.

I don't code. Can I still participate?
Yes. There will be numerous challenges geared towards people with no programming experience.

What did people create last year?
Participants created fully-functioning applications that included:

  • an application for empowering rural farmers with market price and crop related climate data
  • solutions for visualizing Kepler exoplanet data
  • a standardized set of font symbols for meterologists to mark maps digitally
  • an SMS powered night sky observation app that tells you when the space station and other satellites are overhead
