GETTING HERE

The International Space Apps Challenge in New York City will be held at Microsoft HQ at 11 Times Square, conveniently located across from Port Authority. All major train lines will get you here: A, C, E to 42nd Street-Port Authority and 1, 2, 3, N, Q, R, 7 to 42nd Street-Times Square.

HOTEL ACCOMMODATION

Our official hotel partner is YOTEL, New York's most innovative hotel located at 42nd and 10th Ave in Hell's Kitchen. Adopting methods of the airline industry, YOTEL makes a visit to NYC affordable, efficient and fun. Guests will enjoy fast kiosk check-in, a robot to store luggage and a room designed by first class airline architects.

Freebies include muffins, coffee and tea in the morning and Wi-Fi throughout the entire hotel.

A limited number of NON-REFUNDABLE discount rooms are available for registered hackathon participants on a first-come, first-serve basis. Please email spaceappsnyc@gmail.com for details and availability.
